 		Open Positions:
Board of Directors

Secretary: As Secretary of the Board your role is to keep and distribute meeting minutes from monthly board meetings, you'll also be responsible for assembling content, designing, and distributing the monthly PS SIGCHI newsletter.

Vice Chair: As the Vice Chair of the Board, your role is to assist the President with leading PS SIGCHI, provide support for planning, organizing and facilitating Board sponsored events

Social Media Chair: As the Social Media Chair your role is to announce PS SIGCHI's monthly events across our Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn accounts, develop hashtags for use during events, and respond to inquiries from these channels.

Volunteer Coordinator: As the Volunteer Coordinator your role is to recruit and wrangle a willing, eager team of volunteers to help with various areas of need within the organization
How can I get involved?

We are currently looking to fill these positions for our 2014-15 season. If you are interested, please email your resume and letter of intent to pssigchi@gmail.com. Thank you for your interest!
